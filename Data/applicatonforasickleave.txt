Here's a sample application for a sick leave:

**Application for Sick Leave**

To,
[Manager's Name]
[Company Name]
[Date]

Subject: Request for Sick Leave

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to request a sick leave for [number of days] days starting from [date of leave]. Unfortunately, I have fallen ill and am unable to come to work during this period.

I am currently experiencing [briefly mention your symptoms, e.g. fever, body ache, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take some time off to rest and recover, and I will be unable to perform my duties during this period.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on my work as soon as I am fit to return. I will keep you updated on my progress and will notify you as soon as I am ready to return to work.

I would greatly appreciate it if you could approve my request for sick leave. If there is any additional documentation required, please let me know.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
